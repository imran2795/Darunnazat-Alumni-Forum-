About Us

Darunnazat Siddikia Kamil Madrasah Alumni Association (DSKMAA)

DSKMAA is an organization of former students of Dakhil 2020 and Alim 2022 batches of Darunnazat Siddikia Kamil Madrasah. Our goal is to strengthen relationships among all alumni, contribute to the development of educational institutions and participate in social service work.
